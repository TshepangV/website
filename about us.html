About Us

HM Power Vision, a company specializing in student accommodation in the Vaal area in Gauteng province.
​Established in 2012 and founded by Setjhaba Samuel Mokoena​.

Confort Accomodation For Quality Education

HM Power offers communal houses for students around Vaal area from 2012. Our premises are fully equipped to suit your specific needs. We offer single, sharing and en-suite rooms with lots of space. Each room is equipped with bed(s), wardrobe, study table and a chair. All our premises are safe, secure and close to major campuses, this includes VUT, NWU AND Sedibeng College. We also provide free uncapped wifi, Laundry room, communal TV rooms and swimming pool area for recreational purposes.

MISSION

  • To provide conducive accommodation to students of VUT, NWU and surrounding colleges at Vereeniging and Vanderbijlpark.
  • Give them access to resources they need such as WIFI, Laundry area, Beds, Study area, bathrooms, and a fully equipped kitchen to make their lives easy while studying.
  • Provide back-up power during loadshedding.
  • HM Power offers single and shared suites to students depending on their affordability.
  • Provide entertainment area, such as TV rooms, Braai area and Swimming pool.

VISION

HM Power Vision wishes to provide more than 100 students with accommodation. We wish to be able also to provide transportation to institutions as we prioritize safety outside our premises. We wish to create accommodation that makes one feel at home while studying.
